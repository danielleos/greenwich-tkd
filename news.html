14 October 2018

ITFE Championships 2018

The ITF England Championships 2018 will be held on Sunday 28th October at the Royal Masonic Girls' School, Rickmansworth, WD3 4HF. We wish good luck to the members who will be competing and representing Greenwich Taekwon-Do!

14 October 2018

December Grading

The date of the next grading has been confirmed for Saturday 15th December 2018. The exact time has yet to be confirmed, but it will most likely be around 10:30AM and held in the usual training venue - St. Alfege's Church Hall. Please reach out to either Mr Tran or Mr Rose-Trespeuch for more information.
