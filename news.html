3 December 2018

Last Sessions of 2018

Under normal circumstances the last session in December would have been on the 15th this year. However, there is an ETA annual general meeting that day which all instructors must attend. Unfortunately we were unable to secure the hall on Sunday 16th so the last Saturday session before Christmas will be Saturday 8th December with a combined kids class at 9am (slightly earlier to accommodate for the 10am grading) and the additional last Thursday session (to make up for the 15th) will be the 20th of December.

To make up for the missed junior sessions on Saturday 15th we would like to invite anyone who wishes to train to the regular Thursday session on the 13th or the additional Thursday class on the 20th starting at 19:30. In addition to this we will be arranging a kids sparring workshop early January free of charge. Further details to follow.

The grading will commence at 10am on the 8th of December in our regular training hall and will be with Master Dennis. The grading fee is £25 and the grading form is attached to this email, please remember to bring both on the day. The hall will be open from 9am if you would like to arrive early to warm up.

To summarise:

  • Last kids class: December 8th at 9:00, combining both kids classes (with the option to train on 13th or 20th of December at 19:30)
  • Grading: December 8th 10:00-12:00
  • Last adults class: December 20th at 19:30
We hope this all makes sense but please don't hesitate to get in touch with any queries.

11 November 2018

UKTA UK Open 2018

We had 1 competitor, Martin Bowes, representing GTKD at the UKTA UK Open today in Guilford. We are pleased to announce that he managed to bring home a silver medal in the -80kg Veteran Sparring category!

This is a huge achievement as there were 314 competitors from 65 schools throughout the UK (including many international schools). We are extremely proud of this result.

Congratulations once again to you Martin!

29 October 2018

ITF England Championships 2018: Results

We had 4 competitors representing GTKD at the ITF England Championships this Sunday 28th October. We are pleased to announce that we managed to bring home 5 medals!

In the under 14 year-old coloured belts category, Florie Nalepa won bronze medals in both of her events, patterns and sparring and Caspar Nalepa did a fantastic job in patterns as well. In the 18-36 year-old coloured belts category, Ryan Watts won a bronze medal in his sparring event, we are very proud as he did very well going up both a weight class and grade category! Also in the 18-36 coloured belts category, Danielle O'Sullivan came back with two gold medals in patterns and sparring.

We are very proud of our competitors as they all performed extremely well!
