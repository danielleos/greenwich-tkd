About Us

Welcome to Greenwich Taekwon-Do

We aim to promote the Art of Taekwon-Do in Greenwich, East London under the England Taekwon-Do Association which is affiliated to the International Taekwon-Do Federation.

The School runs weekly classes for adults and childen with Instructors Mr Tran (IV Degree) & Mr Rose-Trespeuch (III Degree).

Students at Greenwich Taekwon-Do range from beginner to advanced and new members are very welcome. The first lesson is free of charge, so please come along for your opportunity to try an introductory class in this highly effective self defence.
